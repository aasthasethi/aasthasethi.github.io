Astha Sethi

UI/UX DESIGNER

Born and brought up in Rajasthan(India), I am currently pursuing B.design in Visual Communication and specializing in UserExperience Design from Symbiosis Institute of Design, Pune.

According to me, to design is to make a difference in one's life. Observing right problems, approaching towards them and then solving it is what it takes to be a good experience designer.

User Experience in itself is very vast. I aim for going deeper in terms of the intricacy of user experience design in real life.
